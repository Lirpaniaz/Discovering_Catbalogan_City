Beaches & Resorts in Catbalogan City

La SIERRA ISLAND RESORT

La Sierra Island Resort would like to express our gratitude to our beloved guests who, time and time again, graced us with a myriad of opportunities to host their occasions. Here in La Sierra, celebrations are never-ending, and so we’re extending the party to everyone who has been and are soon to become part of our journey. Located at brgy. Cinco Catbalogan Samar.

M GRAND ROYALE RESORT

M Grand Royale Resort. one of the best places to visit in Catbalogan City. Located inside Executive Heights in Brgy. Guinsorongan, the resort is situated on a hilltop. It has an infinity pool and a restaurant overlooking Maqueda Bay. This place will give you an elegant view of Catbalogan city’s sunset. It is truly an ultimate tourist destination in the city, and it’s just a few minute ride from Catbalogan downtown area.

ROSARIO HILLS VIEW PARK AND RESORT

Brgy. New Mahayag in Catbalogan City, the resort is a great place to go if you have kids with you. The place offers separate swimming pools for adults and children. They also have a children’s playground, which includes kiddie rides, swings, see-saws, monkey bar and slides.

BURI BARAS BEACH

Buri baras beach house is a simple yet cozy,family-owned beachfront home with relaxing garden and view. if you are looking for a traquil amd homey atmosphere,we have the perfect spot for you!! Guests have direct access to the beach, which is just a couple of steps away from the premises.

CAL-APOG BEACH RESORT

Cal Apog Beach Resort, Catbalogan Samar is a beach in Catbalogan.

BANGON FALLS

BANGON FALLS – A 7 layers water falls found in Brgy. San Andress Catbalogan City, Samar. Favorite picnic area by locals. You need to walk 1 hour to reach Bangon falls. But the most adrenaline adventure is Canyoning in Bangon falls were you can go trekking, rappelling ,jumping, swimming, sliding in the water falls

JUVIES RESORT HOTEL AND RESTUARANT

Juvies Resort Hotel and Restaurant offers an airport shuttle (available on request) for PHP 5000 per vehicle roundtrip. After splashing around at one of the 2 outdoor pools, guests can grab a bite to eat at the restaurant. Other highlights include a children's pool, a terrace, and a garden.
